About AdvantageGuide

We help Medicare Advantage members discover and claim the home health benefits their plans already cover.

Our Mission

Most Medicare Advantage members are sitting on benefits they never use. Their plans include allowances for over-the-counter health products, home safety modifications like grab bars and shower chairs, and durable medical equipment coverage that can save hundreds of dollars a year. But the information is buried in dense plan documents, and no one tells members what they have or how to use it.

AdvantageGuide exists to fix that.

Founded by Austin Edy, AdvantageGuide is built on a simple idea: if you are paying for a Medicare Advantage plan, you should actually get everything it covers. We research the benefits, explain them in plain language, and show you the exact steps to claim them. No jargon. No confusion. Just clear answers about what your plan pays for and how to get it.

Whether it is your OTC allowance, a home safety benefit, or DME coverage after a surgery, we want every member to know what is available to them and feel confident using it.

Editorial Standards

We hold ourselves to a high standard because the information we publish affects real decisions about health and money. Here is how we work:

  • Researched from primary sources. All content is based on official plan documents, Summary of Benefits materials, and Medicare.gov. We do not rely on secondhand information or unverified claims.
  • Reviewed for accuracy. Every guide is reviewed for factual accuracy before publication. We check benefit details, coverage amounts, and claiming procedures against current plan information.
  • Updated regularly. Medicare Advantage benefits change from year to year. We revisit and update our guides as plans release new benefit structures so the information stays current.
  • Written in plain language. We believe that if a member cannot understand the explanation, the explanation has failed. Every guide is written to be clear to someone with no background in insurance or healthcare policy.
