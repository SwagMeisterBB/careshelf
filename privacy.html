Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

AdvantageGuide (advantageguide.health) is a free informational website that helps Medicare Advantage members discover and use their plan benefits. We believe in keeping things simple, and that includes how we handle your privacy.

This policy explains what information we collect, what we do not collect, and how we use what we gather.

Information We Collect

We collect very little. AdvantageGuide does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use our site. There are no forms to fill out and no registration process.

The only information we collect automatically is standard web analytics data, which includes:

  • Which pages you visit on our site
  • How long you spend on each page
  • What website or search engine referred you to us
  • Your general geographic region (city or metro area, not your home address)
  • Your device type, browser, and operating system

This information helps us understand which guides are most helpful and how people find our site. It does not identify you personally.

Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics to collect the web analytics data described above. Google Analytics places small text files called cookies on your device to recognize returning visitors and understand general browsing patterns.

These are standard cookies used by millions of websites. They do not contain your name, email address, or any personal details. You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Our site will still work if you disable cookies.

We do not use any advertising trackers, retargeting pixels, or social media tracking tools beyond standard analytics.

Third-Party Links

Our guides include links to products on Amazon. These are affiliate links, which means AdvantageGuide earns a small commission if you purchase a product after clicking one of our links. This does not change the price you pay.

When you click an affiliate link, you leave our website and visit Amazon.com. Once you are on Amazon, their privacy policy governs how your information is handled. We encourage you to review Amazon's privacy policy if you have questions about how they use your data.

We do not receive any personal information about you from Amazon. We only receive anonymous, aggregate reports showing how many people clicked our links and whether purchases were made.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely clear about what we do not collect or store:

  • No personal health information. We never ask about your health conditions, medications, diagnoses, or medical history.
  • No insurance information. We never ask for your Medicare number, plan ID, insurance card details, or any information about your specific coverage.
  • No account data. There are no user accounts on AdvantageGuide. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or mailing address.
  • No payment information. We never process payments or collect credit card numbers.
  • No Social Security numbers. We will never ask for your Social Security number under any circumstances.

If anyone contacts you claiming to be from AdvantageGuide and asks for any of the information listed above, that person is not affiliated with us. Please do not share sensitive information.

Children's Privacy

AdvantageGuide is designed for adults, particularly older adults enrolled in Medicare. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We do not anticipate significant changes because we collect so little information to begin with.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, you can reach us at hello@advantageguide.health.
